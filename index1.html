About Us

Welcome to Future Link Corporation, we provide a platform artists to showcase their work and get paid. Our mission is to empower artists with the right tools and support so they can share their talents with the world.

Our platform is designed to be easy to use and accessible to all, with no contractual binding. We provide the tools and support needed to help artists share their work and get paid for it, while also promoting their talents to a wider audience.

Our team is passionate about helping artists achieve their dreams, and we work tirelessly to ensure that our platform is user-friendly and supportive. We believe that art has the power to change lives, and we're proud to be a part of that change.

Frequently asked questions

What is Future Link and what do you offer?

  • What is Future Link and what do you offer?

    Future Link is more than a platform; we're your creative collaborator. Our skilled artists specialize in crafting custom digital artwork that reflects your unique preferences, transforming your ideas into personalized masterpieces.

  • How does the customization process work at Future Link?

    Bringing your dream artwork to life is a breeze! Start by exploring our Deliveries section on the website, and reach out to us via the Contact section to delve into the specifics of your vision. Commissioning us is seamless through Buy Me a Coffee, where our artists will work their magic. We offer drafts and revisions until your heart resonates with the masterpiece, saying, "This is it!"

  • What types of digital artwork does Future Link offer?

    Future Link opens the door to a world of artistic possibilities. From enchanting illustrations to captivating portraits and whimsical caricatures, explore our portfolio on the Future Link website for inspiration that speaks to you.

  • Can I request revisions to the artwork at Future Link?

    Absolutely! Your satisfaction is our mission at Future Link. We welcome revision requests to ensure the final piece is a perfect representation of your vision.

  • How long does it take to receive my custom digital artwork from Future Link?

    The timeframes at Future Link vary depending on the complexity of the project. Rest assured, our efficient team works tirelessly to deliver high-quality artwork within the agreed-upon timeframe. While the maximum timeframe is six weeks, it's important to note that this isn't a strict limit. The timeline is subject to the availability of the artist and required resources. We strive to expedite deliveries as much as possible.

  • What is the refund policy at Future Link?

    At Future Link, we steadfastly uphold our dedication to customer satisfaction. If your artwork doesn't spark joy, we gladly offer a full refund. Simply reach out to your assigned artist or Contact us directly. We don't ask questions for any refund requests, though your feedback is invaluable in helping us improve our services

  • Is my personal information secure with Future Link?

    Your privacy is sacred at Future Link. We've implemented stringent measures to fortify the security of your personal information. Peek into our privacy policy on the our website for the full scoop.

  • How can I contact customer support at Future Link?

    Need assistance? Our ever-ready customer support team at Future Link is just a message or call away. Reach out through our privacy policy secrion, and we'll swoop in with prompt assistance.

  • What payment methods does Future Link accept?

    Future Link plays nice with various payment methods, Payment section. Your transactions at Future Link are wrapped in layers of security and encryption for your peace of mind.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We only have your personal contact related information, such as your name, email address, and other relevant details, when you interact with our website or services. Any sensitive information related to payments, such as financial or personal data, is collected by your chosen payment methods, including Buy Me a Coffee (BMC), PayPal, or Stripe. For a detailed review of how your payment methods handle sensitive information, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

  • Buy Me a Coffee (BMC) Privacy Policy
  • PayPal Privacy Policy
  • Stripe Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to provide and improve our services, communicate with you, and customize your experience. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of our site.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but strive to safeguard your data.

5. Your Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also opt-out of receiving promotional emails by following the instructions provided in the emails.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the latest revision will be indicated. We encourage you to review this policy regularly for updates.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, Please feel to Contact us.
